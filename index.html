Accessibility plugin for blind gamers

Minecraft

A childhood friend of mine in his recent years was diagnosed with a rare disease that impaired most of his vision. He is still working out what he can and cannot do, but he continues to surprise everyone around him with what he has accomplished. That is until we tried playing Minecraft together. He struggled to read what was in his inventory and craft items he had previously known. Minecraft's native accessibility did not aid his problems and I was mad. I decided to learn java and within a few hours I had made a plugin that read out every item he clicked on. His diamonds continue to stack up.

Tech used: Java
